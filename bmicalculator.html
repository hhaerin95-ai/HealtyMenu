What is BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on your height and weight. It's a useful indicator to assess whether you're at a healthy weight for your height.

While BMI is a helpful screening tool, it doesn't directly measure body fat or muscle mass. For a comprehensive health assessment, consult with a healthcare professional.

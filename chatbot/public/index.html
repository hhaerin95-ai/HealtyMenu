Understanding Your BMI

⚠️ Underweight

BMI < 18.5

What to focus on:

  • Increase calorie intake with nutrient-dense foods
  • Add more protein: chicken, fish, eggs, tofu
  • Healthy fats: avocado, nuts, olive oil
  • Frequent meals: 5-6 small meals daily

🍽️ Recommended Foods:

Nasi LemakRoti Canai Ayam GorengIkan Bakar Telur RebusKacang PisangSusu

✅ Normal Weight

BMI 18.5 - 24.9

What to focus on:

  • Maintain balanced portions
  • Mix of carbs, protein & vegetables
  • Stay active with regular exercise
  • Keep consistent eating schedule

🍽️ Recommended Foods:

Nasi PutihAyam Panggang Sayur CampurIkan Kukus TauhuBuah-buahan Sup SayurOat

⚠️ Overweight

BMI 25.0 - 29.9

What to focus on:

  • Reduce portion sizes gradually
  • Choose grilled over fried foods
  • Increase vegetables and fibre intake
  • Aim for 30 minutes exercise daily

🍽️ Recommended Foods:

Nasi PerangAyam Panggang Ikan KukusSayur Tumis Tauhu RebusBuah Segar OatAir Masak

🚨 Obese

BMI ≥ 30.0

What to focus on:

  • Reduce calorie intake gradually
  • Avoid fried & high-sugar foods
  • More vegetables & lean protein
  • Smaller portions, no late-night eating

🍽️ Recommended Foods:

Nasi MerahAyam Rebus SaladIkan Panggang Sayur RebusTeh O Kosong EpalAir Masak

Healthy Living Tips

🥗

Balanced Diet

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in every meal for optimal nutrition.

💧

Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily. Proper hydration boosts metabolism and energy levels.

🏃

Regular Exercise

Aim for 30 minutes of physical activity daily. Even a brisk walk makes a difference!

😴

Quality Sleep

Get 7-9 hours of sleep each night. Good sleep is crucial for weight management and overall health.

🍬

Limit Sugar

Reduce intake of sugary drinks and processed foods. Choose natural sweeteners when possible.

📏

Portion Control

Use smaller plates and listen to your body's hunger cues. Quality over quantity!

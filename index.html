About This System

Our web-based system is designed to help individuals, especially those managing obesity and diabetes, make informed dietary choices. We provide personalized healthy menu recommendations based on your Body Mass Index (BMI), along with comprehensive nutritional information and food intake tracking.

Whether you're looking to maintain a healthy weight, manage a health condition, or simply eat better, our system offers the guidance and tools you need to succeed.
